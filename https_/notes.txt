Example URL: https://www.example.com/path/to/resource?name=value#section

Scheme: https (secure protocol)
Domain: www.example.com (website address)
Path: /path/to/resource (specific resource location)
Query: ?name=value (optional parameters)
Fragment: #section (specific part of the resource)
This URL accesses a secure resource on a website, with parameters and a specific section identifier.
